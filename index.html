The Poem

    			This server marks another year,
    			And now treasure abounds,
    			But to find if it's buried here,
    			You must now trek these grounds.

    			Begin where demand suddenly drops cold,
    			And take it to the village down.
    			Not far, but much too far to hold.
    			Placed beneath that un-boom town.

    			From here there is no city to speak,
    			Charted across the waters cold.
    			But if to find what you shall seek,
    			Head towards the largest marble gold.

    			If you've been wise and found the pool,
    			Jump in, and let your search be done!
    			Dig two down before this yule,
    			And announce the treasure now is none!

    			And even with these longest clues
    			You cannot the treasure find,
    			Listen close to my news,
    			And use those hints assigned.

    			For now! my time here comes to an end,
    			My treasure for the world to seek.
    			Six stacks of blocks for you to spend,
    			So take them from this mighty creek.
